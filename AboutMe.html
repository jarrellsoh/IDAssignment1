About Me

See the highest guy in the picture? That's me! This picture features my ODAC CCA members back when I was Secondary 2, and when I did not have much experience in the CCA. Now that I've graduated from Maris Stella High School, my Secondary School, I look back and I realise that ODAC was one of the best things that happened in my life so far. It taught me many soft skills like Determination, Perseverance and Teamwork. Now that I'm studying Information Technology in Ngee Ann Polytechnic, these values help me a huge amount whenever I do assignments, study for tests etc.

As for now, I am a National Athlete for Sport Climbing, while being a full-time student. While it might be tough to cope sometimes, I have never regretted making this decision as it has taught me many different things including time management and nutrition among other things. Nevertheless, I hope you enjoy looking through my portfolio and feel free to leave some feedback in the last page using the Contact Me button!
